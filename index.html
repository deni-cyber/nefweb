About Us

North Edge Fabricators Limited is a dynamic and reliable company specializing in quality metal fabrication and engineering solutions. Strategically located at Kariobangi Light Industries, Nairobi, we are committed to delivering durable, efficient, and innovative products that meet the diverse needs of our clients across different industries.

Our Core Business

At North Edge Fabricators Limited, we specialize in a wide range of metal fabrication and engineering solutions tailored to meet the needs of individuals, companies, and institutions. Our core areas include:
• Custom Metal Fabrication Works
• Street Lighting Poles Fabrication and Installation
• Doors and Window Frames Fabrication
• Water Tanks and Towers Fabrication
• High Masts Fabrication and Installation
