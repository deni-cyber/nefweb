About us

North Edge Fabricators Limited is a trusted provider of high-quality metal fabrication and engineering solutions. Based at Kariobangi Light Industries, Nairobi, we are committed to precision, durability, and innovation in every project. Our expertise enables us to deliver customised solutions that support the growth and efficiency of businesses across multiple industries.
