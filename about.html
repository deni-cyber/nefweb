About us

North Edge Fabricators Limited is a trusted provider of high-quality metal fabrication and engineering solutions. Based at Kariobangi Light Industries, Nairobi, we are committed to precision, durability, and innovation in every project. Our expertise enables us to deliver customised solutions that support the growth and efficiency of businesses across multiple industries.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, innovative, and cost-effective fabrication solutions that help our clients achieve their project goals on time and to the highest standards.

Our Vision

To be a leading fabricator and supplier of durable and innovative engineering products in Kenya and beyond.

Our Values

  • Quality Commitment to excellence in every product and service.
  • Innovation Continuous improvement and adoption of modern fabrication technologies.
  • Integrity Transparent and ethical business practices.
  • Customer Focus Tailored solutions that meet unique client needs.
  • Sustainability Environmentally responsible and efficient operations.
